Jesus Light of the World Christian Ministries

Founded by the late Bro Art and Sister Thelma Aguinaldo at their residential house in Tabon Kawit, Cavite. Built the amazing grace of God, thru and by faith of the two Bretheren, they establish the community of Jesus Light of the World Christian Ministries. For the remembrance of our beloved Bro Art & Sister Thelma & for the serviceon our Lord Jesus Christ, we continue the legacy of faith they imparted on to us. May the Name of the Lord Jesus Christ be exalted, be praise and be honored.
